The CIA Triad



The CIA Triad is commonly mistaken for the government agency with the same name. However, the CIA Triad represents

3 core pillars in Cybersecurity needed to protect information effectively. The pillars in the triad are:


Confidentiality

Confidentiality means protecting data so only authorized people can access it. This can be done with tools

such as passwords, encryption, and access controls to keep information private.

Integrity

Integrity is about making sure data is accurate and not altered by unauthorized people. Checks such as digital

signatures and version control help ensure that the data stays correct over time.

Availability

Availability means making sure data and systems are available to those who need it. Regular maintenance, backups,

and secure systems help keep information accessible, even during problems like power outages or cyberattacks.

Importance of the CIA Triad

By following these three principles, organizations can protect data, follow legal requirements, and reduce security risks.
